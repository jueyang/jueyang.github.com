I used to call this page: blog. I wrote when I felt I had something to say, though I was not always good at saying them. As a result, there were varying degrees of pompousness, obliqueness and typos in the earlier proses. I started writing here again in 2020. Time has passed; both the world and I have changed. One of these changes is the page title, which is now: thoughts.
