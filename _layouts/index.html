Jue Yang: An Introduction

I like words and space. I guess you can call me one of the following based on your preferred framework: trans-media writer, writer for performance, playwright.

Currently intrigued by: theatre before Theater, the death of the artist, non-capitalist systems, structure of the body, ontology, monism, small public spaces, paper.

Identities in Transition

I have three portfolios summarizing my education and experiences as a working being. This includes being a cartographer, an aspiring architect, and a technologist (keywords: data, design, develop). Each one was created when I looked for the next job. As a writer, I have been re-evaluating such notion of a "working being". What kind of way of life is that? In 2015, before I had words to confidently answer that, I wrote a play for myself to figure it out.
