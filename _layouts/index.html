Jue Yang: An Introduction

I am an artist, trans-media writer, playwright.

I am intrigued by: emergence, empathy, expedition, language, meditation, memory, ontology, time, truth.

Below is a brief introduction to my practice and trajectory. For the impatient reader, here is my cv.

The Present

Having considered text as my main medium, I am now enrolled in a Master's progam in Lens-based Media at the Piet Zwart Institute.

In this portfolio I summarize the direction of my work that has led me here.

I live in Rotterdam, the Netherlands.
