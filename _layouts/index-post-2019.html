In May 2020:

The world is still moving at a strange pace.

She updated her cirriculum vitae.*

She finished a memoir.

She does not want to stop writing.

She unearths a blog from what seems to be decades ago — which is, really, oceans away — and starts adding new pieces to it.

Her email is still lemony.space@gmail.com

(She awaits for the stars.)

* They say it is the course of life.
Not much has changed but drips of time pooling
into an existence forgotten by the one who inhabits it.
They say the archive has a narrative.

Come back in a month or two. This place is changing.

May you be safe.

She writes this in the Netherlands, signing:

Yours Truly,

Yours,

(And they do rise.)
